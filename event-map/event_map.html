Selected conflicts in the British Empire -- ca. 1500 to 1819

The colour scheme reflects different types of events which can be (de)selected in the filters. The categories are subject to interpretation and simplification of the insurgents' motives. Event descriptions in the labels are based on Wikidata / Wikipedia entries or item abstracts provided by the National Archives of the UK. Sources are indicated in the item labels. Checking and updating the data is an on-going process. The current version of the geocoded data can be downloaded in GeoJSON format.
The MAGNIFYING GLASS permits searches for locations by place name. Clicking on a place name in the dropdown menu re-centers the map accordingly.
